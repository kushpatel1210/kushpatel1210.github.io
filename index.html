About Umiya Physiotherapy Center

At Umiya Physiotherapy Center, we are dedicated to providing the highest quality of care to help our patients regain their mobility, reduce pain, and improve their overall quality of life.

Our experienced and compassionate team utilizes evidence-based practices and state-of-the-art equipment to create personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific needs. We believe in empowering our patients through education and active participation in their recovery journey.

Our Specialized Physiotherapy Services

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Specialized treatment for musculoskeletal injuries, fractures, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Comprehensive care for conditions like stroke, Parkinson's, and spinal cord injuries.

Pediatric Physiotherapy

Dedicated therapy for children with developmental delays and movement disorders.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Expert care for athletes to recover from injuries and optimize performance.

Women's Health Physiotherapy

Specialized treatments for pre and postnatal care, pelvic floor dysfunction, and more.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Assistance for seniors to maintain mobility, balance, and independence.
